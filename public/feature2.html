THE PROJECT

The purpose of the project was to refresh an old out of date website for the Thames Anglers Rivers Association while maintaining the original brands identity, keeping their logo and content etc.

WHAT I DID

During this job I had to learn how to collaberate effectively and worked alot on my time management skills. This also really taught me the fundamentals of coding and best workplace and industry standards.

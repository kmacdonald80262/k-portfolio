CLIENT

safe & well

THE PROJECT

While working at my internship at sagecomm, I started working on a project for their client to run a campaign we called 'safe and well'. The campaign was for the Middle-Sex County to use, they were to use all the same advertising and socials to create a unified front where the goal was to get the community more involved and in touch with the healthcare providers and police officers keeping their community safe; so they could freely give suggestions and keep the conversation open.

WHAT I DID

My job for this campaign was to create some digital ads for social media, twitter, facebook, instagram, and linked-in. I created 3 different versions for each and then also created posters and other web banners etc including some items for print as well.

During this job I had to make sure I was always on task and ready with multiple different versions of every sizing my client wanted, as well as handling the flow of changes and updates to each advertisement.
